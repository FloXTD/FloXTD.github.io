What is AI?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) refers to the simulation of human intelligence in machines that are programmed to think and learn like humans. AI can be applied in various fields such as healthcare, finance, education, and more.

Significance of AI

AI has the potential to revolutionize industries by improving efficiency, accuracy, and decision-making processes. It enables automation of repetitive tasks and provides insights through data analysis.
